Luna returned to Yosemite for her second visit, this time as she and I joined with Cara’s lab. We celebrated some recent achievements, enjoyed the grandeur of the Yosemite Valley, and disconnected for a few days. Now that Luna was a bit older, she got to walk around the Valley and met many new friends. She especially enjoyed snuggling with her humans in the tent!
